Greent Pet

UI / UX / game
Description

No kidding, this is my Master degree's graduation project. The goal of the proeject is to using plants as gameplay input and test player's perception of interactions at different levels. The peoject is instructed by Dr. Jina Huh and Dr. Taiwoo Park, co-developed with Jake Lange (programming) and Jackson Hopcrpft (art).

Intro

Originally I was planning to make something to help people better take care of their plants, while in the interview and literature review stage, I found people usually feel peaceful and relaxed when spending time with their plants, which led the proejct to a more interesting direction.

Reflection

Due to the late switch of project design, as well as the technical challenges in electrical engineering part, I have experience huge pressure and anxiety during the project. But with things getting decomposed into pieces, helps from professors and other resources, I see this project will put a very proud ending to my 2 years' study in MSU.
